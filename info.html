What is this archive about?

This is an ongoing record and free-to-use checklist of the whimsical bugs that can be found in the Art Building on campus!

How do I use it?

Underneath each identifying image there is a checkbox for your convenience and an empty text entry area to record your findings while you hunt around for these bugs.

Where is the Art Building?

Look for a U-shaped, orange building on the south side of the Student Union!
