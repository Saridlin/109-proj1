The Fantastical Bugs of the SJSU Art Building

Have you ever noticed the cute, little critters crawling in the Art Building before?
These fascinating, but rare creatures show up once in a while in the San Jose State University’s Art Building.
Use this archive to track your findings and lead you to finding even more interesting bugs in between your busy schedule and walking to class!

The Busy Body Bee



Beezy librarius

Tucked away on the second floor of the art building is a quiet, book-filled space where a barely audible buzzing can sometimes be heard. With the enormous quantity of artbooks stored away in this room, there are plenty of places for this busy body bee to hide! It takes a fascination in organizing and archiving the robust collection.

Need a hint?

Some say that catching a glimpse of this adorable little librarian helps you in your studies! Keep a look out for any glittering as it’s easiest to spot this critter by its holographic wings. “Bee” careful not to bring any liquids or dangers to the library room or face the wrath of this hard worker.

The Modest Moth




Mothim timodum

Unlike common moths, this species sports a plethora of vibrant patterning on its body. Even with its unusual beauty, this moth tends to shy away from attention and secludes itself. It is an enormous labor to spot this critter during the day. However, if you ever manage to spot the cerulean rarity, be sure to not use flashy cameras or sudden movements in your observation.

Need a hint?

It may be easiest to get a look at this flying beauty in the dim twilights or in quiet, sight-seeing areas of the building. It tends to be just as elusive as any art student who is coming up on assignment deadlines.

The Entrepreneurial Worm




Wurm bizonis

Have you ever wondered who keeps the pins on the poster boards stocked or invests in the art department? This jovial fellow sure knows! A hard worker and knowledgeable worm, this entrepreneur helps the department administration in any minutia both figuratively and literally. This worm may also be responsible for the timely upkeep of the building’s security and door locks.

Need a hint?

Look out for any comfortable windowsill seating areas. Every shrewd business person needs a peaceful break in their busy schedule to relax with a cup of tea by a pleasant window outlook.
